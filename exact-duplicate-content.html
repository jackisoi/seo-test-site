About Our Company

Founded in 2005, Greenfield Technologies has been at the forefront of sustainable innovation for nearly two decades. Our journey began in a small garage in Portland, Oregon, where three passionate engineers decided to build products that would make a real difference in how businesses approach environmental responsibility. Today, we operate in twelve countries and employ over two thousand dedicated professionals who share our vision of a cleaner, more efficient world.

Our mission is simple yet ambitious: to provide cutting-edge technological solutions that help organizations reduce their environmental footprint without sacrificing performance or profitability. We believe that sustainability and business success are not mutually exclusive, and our track record proves it. Over the past eighteen years, our clients have collectively reduced their carbon emissions by an estimated forty percent while increasing operational efficiency by an average of twenty-five percent.

At Greenfield Technologies, we are committed to continuous improvement and open collaboration. We invest heavily in research and development, dedicating approximately fifteen percent of our annual revenue to exploring new materials, processes, and technologies. Our partnerships with leading universities and research institutions around the world ensure that we remain at the cutting edge of sustainable innovation, delivering solutions that are not only effective today but prepared for the challenges of tomorrow.
